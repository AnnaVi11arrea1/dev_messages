01

Introduction

Welcome to our messaging application ("App", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights with respect to that information.

By using our App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

02

Information We Collect

Message Content

Our App collects and processes the messages and content you send and receive. This includes text messages, images, files, and any other media you share through the messaging features. All message content is protected by end-to-end encryption.

Technical Data

We may automatically collect limited technical data necessary to operate the App, such as device type, operating system version, app version, and crash or diagnostic reports. This data never includes message content.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To deliver messages between you and your contacts.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the App.
  • To diagnose technical problems and fix bugs.
  • To comply with applicable laws and legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information or message content to third parties. We do not use your messages for advertising purposes.

04

End-to-End Encryption

Your privacy is our highest priority. Our App uses end-to-end encryption (E2EE) to ensure that only you and your intended recipient can read your messages.

How Our Encryption Protects You

Every message is secured before it leaves your device — we can't read it even if we wanted to.

  • Encryption keys are generated and stored only on your device, never on our servers.
  • Messages in transit are fully encrypted and unreadable to us or any third party.
  • In the event of a server breach, your message content remains protected.
  • We cannot recover your messages if you lose access to your device or account.
05

Data Retention

Messages are stored only as long as necessary to deliver them to the recipient, and are deleted from our servers upon successful delivery. We do not maintain archives of your message content on our servers.

Technical and diagnostic data may be retained for up to 12 months for operational purposes, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

06

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited technical data only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who assist in operating the App (e.g., cloud infrastructure), under strict confidentiality obligations and only to the extent necessary.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or legal process, and only to the extent required. Due to E2EE, we cannot provide message content even if compelled.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger or acquisition, subject to equivalent privacy protections for your information.

08

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will promptly delete it.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately using the details in Section 10.

09

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including end-to-end encryption for all message content, secure transmission protocols (TLS), strict access controls for internal systems, and regular security assessments.

While we strive to protect your information, no security system is impenetrable. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and enable any available security features within the App.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the App at least 30 days before those changes take effect.
